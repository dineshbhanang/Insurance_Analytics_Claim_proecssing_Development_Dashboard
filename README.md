# 🛡️ Insurance Claims & Policy Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Insurance Claims & Policy Analytics Dashboard** is a comprehensive Power BI solution developed to provide insurance companies with a centralized view of policy performance, premium collections, claims processing, customer insights, and operational efficiency. The dashboard transforms complex insurance data into meaningful business insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators, optimize claim settlements, and improve customer satisfaction through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Insurance companies manage thousands of policies and claims across multiple products, branches, and customer segments. Traditional reporting methods often make it difficult to monitor claim processing, premium collections, policy renewals, and business performance in real time.

This dashboard consolidates policy, customer, and claims data into a single interactive platform, allowing management to identify operational bottlenecks, monitor business KPIs, reduce claim turnaround time, and improve overall decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall insurance business performance.
- Analyze premium collection and policy growth.
- Track claim settlement and rejection trends.
- Evaluate customer and policy distribution.
- Monitor branch and regional performance.
- Identify high-value customers and claim patterns.
- Improve operational efficiency through interactive analytics.
